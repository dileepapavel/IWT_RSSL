Our History

Founded in 1991 at Road Saviors Sri Lanka we pride ourselves on offering an innovative alternative to what you may already be used to. You can expect a nationwide car breakdown cover service an at outstanding value, backed up with products you can count on! Our customers' safety and well-being are our goal, so you know you can rely on the Road Saviors Sri Lanka team to send the right help you need, when you need it!

Our Vision

For more than 100 years, it has been Road Savior’s mission to prevent accidents, disease and emergency situations, to rescue and assist people in emergencies quickly and competently and to rehabilitate people after illness and injury.

Our Mission

Based on this mission, it is Road Savior’s vision to develop a major international organisation working within Assistance.​
